As a prospective tenant, I can:
create an account
view open apartments
schedule a viewing
cancel a viewing

As a tenant, I can:
log in
view bills
put in a maintenance ticket
view past maintenance tickets
view (other) open apartments
post complaints

As a manager, I can:
view open/occupied apartments
override bills
manage tenant complaints

As a maintenance person, I can:
view open tickets
log resolutions
charge users for actions taken
